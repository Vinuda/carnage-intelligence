Executive Brief

Market Intelligence — Q2 2025 Snapshot

Carnage, founded by Joel Tillekeratne, has achieved a Net Sentiment Score of +84—placing it exceptionally high among both local and emerging global activewear brands at its stage. Its launch positioning has resonated strongly with Sri Lanka's premium fitness demographic, driven by a distinct visual identity, scarcity-led drops, and authentic community-first engagement. This is not accidental; it is the result of deliberate brand architecture.

In the domestic landscape, Carnage outperforms Envoy Sport and FOA on perceived quality and hype metrics, while commanding a price premium that local competitors cannot yet credibly challenge. On a global axis, Carnage occupies a strategic white-space: lifestyle-oriented performance wear priced accessibly against Lululemon, yet premium against budget local alternatives. The primary risk surface is community scalability—sustaining the intimacy of early-adopter engagement as the brand grows. The data below provides a full-spectrum breakdown to inform product, positioning, and growth decisions.
